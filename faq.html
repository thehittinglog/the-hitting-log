Everything you need to know about The Hitting Log.

The Hitting Log is a baseball and softball development app that helps players, parents, and coaches track every at-bat and uncover meaningful trends over time. Instead of simply recording batting average, The Hitting Log measures approach, timing, pitch recognition, contact quality, and much more to help players improve.

The app is designed for baseball and softball players of all ages, from youth athletes just beginning their journey to high school players preparing for college recruiting. Parents, private instructors, and coaches can also use the app to monitor development over time.

Rather than focusing on batting average alone, the HLP Score rewards quality at-bats, approach, timing, and consistent improvement.
